I've made too many player moevement scripts to count, so this part was relatively easier compared to the rest. I made an InputActions asset, and hooked it up to a basic movement script. The only thing different was, flipping my character whenever I move in the opposite way and making sure that the x, y, and z rotations of my character are fixed since i'm making a 2.5D game,
